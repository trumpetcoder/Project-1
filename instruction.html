Game Instructions

  • This is a two Player Game
  • There is a hidden Chest of Gold!
  • The Player has a total of 36 clicks
  • Each Player goes until he finds the Chest of Gold
  • Click on a square in the Game Board to see if it's there
  • The Player with the least amount of clicks to find the Gold wins!
  • If players are tied noone wins
  • Press the reset button to start a new game
  • Good Luck and may the winds of furtone be kind!
